How to Wash

To ensure your woven clothes maintain their quality and last longer, please follow these gentle care instructions:

  • Hand Wash Only: We recommend hand washing your woven clothes to ensure gentle treatment of the delicate fibers and intricate designs.
  • Use Mild Detergent: When washing, use a mild detergent specifically designed for delicate fabrics. Avoid harsh chemicals or bleach, as they can damage the fibers and colors.
  • Cold Water Wash: Wash your woven garments in cold water to prevent shrinking and preserve the fabric's integrity.
  • Gentle Handling: Handle your woven clothes with care during washing. Avoid vigorous rubbing or wringing, as this can distort the shape and texture of the fabric.
  • Air Dry: After washing, air dry your woven clothes by laying them flat on a clean towel. Avoid using a dryer, as the heat can damage delicate fibers.
  • Avoid Direct Sunlight: When drying, place your woven clothes away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading and fabric damage.
  • Iron with Caution: If ironing is necessary, use a low heat setting and place a cloth over the garment to protect delicate fibers.
  • Store with Care: Store your clean, dry woven clothes in a cool, dry place. Avoid hanging heavy garments on delicate woven fabrics to prevent stretching.

Following these simple care instructions will help preserve the beauty and longevity of your woven clothes, ensuring you can enjoy them for years to come.
